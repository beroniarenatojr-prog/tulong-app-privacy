Privacy Policy for Tulong App

Last updated: December 26, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Tulong App collects the following information to provide our community helper services:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and profile photo
  • Location Data: General location (barangay/city) to match help requests with nearby volunteers
  • Help Requests: Details of help requests you create or respond to
  • Photos: Images you upload related to help requests (optional)
  • Communication: Messages and notifications within the app

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Connect people who need help with available volunteers
  • Send notifications about help requests and responses
  • Maintain user profiles and ratings
  • Ensure community safety and prevent misuse
  • Improve our services

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only:

  • With other users when you create or respond to help requests
  • With local officials (barangay/city admins) for community coordination
  • When required by law or to protect safety

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We use Firebase security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Secure authentication
  • Regular security updates
  • Access controls and monitoring

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Control notification settings
  • Opt out of location sharing

6. Children's Privacy

Tulong App is intended for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information immediately.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we remove your personal information within 30 days, except for data required for legal compliance.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or email.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@tulongapp.com
  • Through the app's support feature

10. Third-Party Services

Tulong App uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication, database, and storage
  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and updates

These services have their own privacy policies which govern their use of your data.

This privacy policy is effective as of December 26, 2025, and applies to all users of Tulong App.
